instructions

Think you can multitask well? Try out your luck in multitaskilus.

You will be presented with some tasks to do. Each task has a timer on it.

Just complete the tasks before their timers run out.

This game is spelled multitaskilus, not multitaskelus, by the way.

controls

Assisted selecting allows you to select text boxes by hovering over them.

Difficulty only affects the length of words that need to be typed.

Press enter to submit a task (unless it's pushing a button).

Make sure cookies are turned on to save your progress.
